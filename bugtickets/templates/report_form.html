Using snAPP's reporting system

At snAPP, we prioritise addressing the problems which are being experienced by the most members. This is decided through a voting system.

It's very simple.

If you experience a bug, search existing bug reports.

Chances are someone else is experiencing the same thing and has reported it already.

You can do this by either scrolling through the list of bugs on this page or, more efficiently, search by a keyword (e.g. 'login', 'crash'). Click on the bug's title to find out more details.

If this is the case,'Upvote' the report and feel free to leave a comment.

If nobody has reported the problem yet, complete a Report Form.

snAPP admin will notify you in the comments when work has begun fixing the problem and/or may leave a comment seeking more details about the problem you are experiencing.

Report effectively

When reporting a bug, please be as specific as possible. We will sort out the issue quicker if the information provided is both accurate and clear.

Please follow these guidelines: Title: Provide a short title of your choice which sums up the problem you are experiencing.

Bug type: Select the heading from the dropdown list which you believe your bug falls under:

Functional - something that you expect snAPP to do is hard, awkward, confusing, or impossible
Communication - it is not clear how to use a feature of snAPP
Syntax - spelling and/or grammar is poor or confusing
Error Notices - you experience an error notice when using snAPP
Calculation Errors - the number of upvotes, comments, items in your checkout or total amount is wrong
Flow Problems - the navigation of snAPP is not brigning you where you want it to

Description: Describe your problem in the most concise and accurate way you can. It helps us to solve issues quickly!
